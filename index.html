Selected from recent 2024–2025 market reports, HMDA actions, and infrastructure plans. Top localities: Kokapet, Gachibowli, Nanakramguda/Raidurg, Shadnagar, Miyapur.

Kokapet

Financial district / premium land demand
Kokapet has continued investor interest and premium land prices due to its proximity to the Financial District, ORR connectivity and recent HMDA actions. (Demo summary)

Gachibowli

IT & education hub — strong rental demand
A mature IT hub with steady capital appreciation and rental yields. Good for salaried buyers and rental-focused investors.
